Company Overview

Founded and owned by Khethelo Litha Ntombela, Litha Ntombela Enterprises is a dynamic logistics brokerage firm based in Gauteng, South Africa. We specialize in outsourcing a fleet of trucks to serve diverse industries with reliable and cost-effective transportation solutions.

As a 100% black-owned company with B-BBEE Level 1 certification, we are committed to empowering local businesses and strengthening supply chain operations across the region.

In addition to logistics, we are expanding into pig and cow farming as part of our commitment to sustainable agribusiness and food security in South Africa.

Our Services

  • Logistics Brokerage: Connecting businesses with transport providers.
  • Freight Facilitation: Ensuring secure and timely goods movement.
  • Customized Transport Solutions: Tailored logistics for specific client needs.
  • Supply Chain Coordination: Streamlining operations for efficiency.

Business Expansion

We are proud to announce our move into livestock farming, including pig and cow production. This expansion aims to support agricultural development, create employment, and contribute to food security in South Africa.
